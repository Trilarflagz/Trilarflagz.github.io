Jingqi Tian (田景绮)

Hi!I am a first-year master at Tsinghua University, majoring in Data Science and Information Technology(DSIT). I expect to graduate in 2028. I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Yansong Tang in IVG@SZ group.

Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Sichuan University in 2025. Before that, I worked as an intern in TUDB-Labs advised by Prof. Mingjie Tang, doing research in the field of LLM systems and algorithms.

My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), such as Multimodal Learning, image Segmentation, Tracking.
